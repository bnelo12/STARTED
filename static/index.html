WHAT IS START|ED?

Interested in working in Start-Ups?

Introducing Start-ED; the first student led event with the aim of opening more doors to students after graduation. Our programmes are designed and will be ran by 30+ different start-ups; with the aim of equipping you with the right tools and connections to work in start-ups.

And, you will also get the chance to meet our guests from local and international start ups, incubators and venture capitals.

Here’s a taster of what we have lined up for you:

  • workshops

  • keynotes

  • networking sessions

  • mentoring sessions

The following are some of our guests/partners :

Give a Grad a Go, RASA.ai, LaunchED, Mallzee, Edinburgh Careers, Appointedd, Future X, Social Bite, Autopsy.io, BrewGooder, Saltire, School Lab (French Incubator), Administrate, Scotland IS, Swipii, Lil Pop, Compass News, Neatebox, Co + Co, Float App, Uuni, Edinburgh Careers, Adopt and Intern, Float, Nameydo, Fundzing, Whichit, Tuki Travel

So why not get yourself ready to work for start-ups?

Start - ED was conceived and ran by a conjoined team of students from two of Edinburgh’s largest universities - Heriot Watt and University of Edinburgh. We started with the vision of getting students prepared and spreading awareness about working in dynamic environments that startups offer.
